Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our clinic, treatments, and policies.

What are your clinic operating hours?

We are open Monday through Saturday from 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM. Sunday consultations are available by prior appointment only. We recommend scheduling in advance for minimal wait times.

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

While we do accommodate walk-in patients when possible, we strongly recommend booking an appointment to ensure dedicated attention from our specialists and minimal waiting time.

Are your procedures painful?

We prioritize patient comfort in every procedure. Our specialists use advanced anesthesia techniques and modern equipment designed for minimally invasive treatment. Many patients with significant dental anxiety report positive, pain-free experiences.

Is the clinic suitable for children?

Absolutely. We have a dedicated pediatric dentistry service with specialists experienced in treating young patients. Our child-friendly approach ensures a positive and comfortable experience for children of all ages.

What is the duration of a root canal procedure?

Using our advanced rotary endodontic systems, most root canal procedures are completed within 30–60 minutes per session, typically requiring 1–2 visits depending on the complexity and location of the affected tooth.

Do you offer flexible payment options?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans including EMI options for major treatment procedures. Our front desk team will be happy to discuss payment arrangements during your consultation.

How frequently should I schedule dental check-ups?

We recommend professional dental examinations and cleaning every six months. Regular visits allow early detection of potential issues and help maintain optimal oral health long-term.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

For dental emergencies — severe pain, trauma, swelling, or a knocked-out tooth — contact us immediately at +91 83308 83232. We prioritize emergency cases and will arrange the earliest available appointment.
